About Me
I currently work as an Instructional Assistant in a Special Education ASD class at Westview High School. I've also worked in Critical Skills classrooms at Westview, and have had experience working with other students with a wide variety of skill levels. I'm motivated and organized in my efforts to encourage students to meet their educational goals and gain independence and confidence.
